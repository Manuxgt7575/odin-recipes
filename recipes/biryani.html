Biryani Recipes

Ingredients

Marinade:

  1. 10 black peppercorns
  2. 6 whole cloves
  3. 5 cardamom pods
  4. 2 cinnamon sticks
  5. 2 whole star anise pods
  6. ½ teaspoon kala jeera (black cumin seeds)
  7. 1 bunch fresh cilantro leaves
  8. 1 bunch fresh mint leaves
  9. 1 cup plain yogurt
  10. 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  11. 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  12. 2 teaspoons chili powder
  13. 1 teaspoon biryani masala powder
  14. ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
  15. 1 pound chicken thighs

Biryani:

  1. 3 ½ cups water
  2. 2 ⅓ cups basmati rice
  3. 4 bay leaves, divided
  4. ½ cup warm milk
  5. 1 pinch saffron threads
  6. ¼ cup ghee, divided
  7. 2 onions, thinly sliced
  8. 2 green chile peppers, chopped
